About Comic Hill

  • What is Comic Hill?

    Comic Hill is a new way to read your favorite webcomics and discover new ones.
    It is also a Java web application built off the Play! Framework and hosted on playapps.net.

  • What isn't Comic Hill?

    I do not produce any of the content that is displayed through this site, other than the application itself.
    All works are copyright their respective owners.

  • Hey, why don't you have this webcomic that I like?

    The comics that I add are all hand-picked. I will do my best to add as much variety as possible, but sometimes there may be technical problems that stand in the way.
    If there is a comic that you would like to see added, let me know!

  • Something doesn't work!

    DON'T PANIC. But please do let me know so I can fix it! Contact me

  • This page design hurts my eyes. Waah!

    I don't claim to be an artist. The design you see today is only temporary.
    If you have suggestions how the design could be improved, please suggest!

  • So, it's an RSS Reader?

    It's true, the functionality here is similar to that of an RSS reader. There are, however, many good reasons that I've chosen this approach.
    1. I don't write webcomics. By using iframes, the author's work is shared without sacrificing the ad revenue that keeps them running.
    2. You get the FULL experience (comments, news, likes, +1's, etc.)
    3. I wanted to write a webapp!

  • Who are you?

    I am a college student who enjoys a good webcomic. Feel free to contact me.
